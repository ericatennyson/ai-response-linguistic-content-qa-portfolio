# Sample 01: B2B Content Clarity Review

## Original Copy

Join our free webinar and learn everything about digital transformation.

## Quality Rating

Needs Improvement

## Issue

The copy is too broad. “Learn everything about digital transformation” is unrealistic and does not clearly communicate the target audience, topic, or practical value of the webinar.

## Improved Copy

Free webinar: How retail teams can use customer data to improve promotions and store operations.

## Rationale

The revised copy is stronger because it clarifies the audience, topic, and business value. It also sets a more realistic expectation for the webinar. For B2B content, specificity is important because users need to quickly understand whether the content is relevant to their role or industry.

## QA Criteria

- Clarity: Improved
- Audience fit: Stronger after revision
- Value proposition: Stronger after revision
- Tone: More credible
- Content usefulness: Improved
