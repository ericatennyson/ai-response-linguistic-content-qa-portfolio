# Sample 02: Landing Page Message Match Review

## Ad or Entry Message

Get a free checklist for improving your e-commerce conversion rate.

## Landing Page Message

Book a paid consultation with our digital marketing team.

## Quality Rating

Poor

## Issue

The entry message promises a free checklist, but the landing page immediately promotes a paid consultation. This creates a mismatch between the user’s expectation and the landing page experience.

## Suggested Fix

The landing page should clearly provide the free checklist near the top of the page. A consultation CTA can still appear later, after the user receives the promised resource.

## Rationale

Message consistency is important for trust and conversion quality. If users click expecting a free checklist but land on a paid consultation page, they may feel misled and leave quickly.

## QA Criteria

- Landing page match: Weak
- User expectation alignment: Not met
- Trust risk: High
- Conversion intent: Misaligned
- Suggested priority: Align the landing page with the original promise
