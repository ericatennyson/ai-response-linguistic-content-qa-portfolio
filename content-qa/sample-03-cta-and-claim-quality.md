# Sample 03: CTA and Claim Quality Review

## Original Copy

Supercharge your workflow with the most powerful AI tool ever. Try it today!

## Quality Rating

Needs Improvement

## Issue

The copy is grammatically correct, but the message is too broad. It does not explain the product category, target audience, or specific workflow benefit. The phrase “most powerful AI tool ever” is exaggerated and unsupported.

## Improved Copy

Draft customer support replies faster with an AI writing assistant built for small teams.

## Rationale

The revised copy is more specific and trustworthy. It identifies the use case, target audience, and practical benefit. It also avoids an exaggerated claim and gives users a clearer reason to click or continue reading.

## QA Criteria

- CTA clarity: Acceptable
- Claim quality: Improved
- Relevance: Improved
- Content clarity: Improved
- Conversion intent: Stronger after revision
