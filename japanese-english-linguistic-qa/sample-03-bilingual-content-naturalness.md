# Sample 03: Bilingual Content Naturalness QA

## Source Text

Download now and discover how leading retailers are improving customer engagement.

## Literal Translation

今すぐダウンロードして、主要な小売業者が顧客エンゲージメントを改善している方法を発見しましょう。

## Improved Japanese

有力小売企業の顧客接点強化策を、今すぐ資料で確認する

## Issue

The literal translation sounds unnatural and too English-like. 「発見しましょう」 and 「顧客エンゲージメントを改善」 are not the most effective phrasings for Japanese B2B content.

## Rationale

The improved version uses more natural Japanese business and marketing language. 「顧客接点強化策」 is more concise and audience-appropriate than a direct translation of “customer engagement.” The action is also clearer with 「資料で確認する」.

## QA Criteria

- Japanese naturalness: Improved
- Bilingual meaning transfer: Preserved
- Tone fit: Improved
- CTA clarity: Improved
- Content usefulness: Stronger after revision
