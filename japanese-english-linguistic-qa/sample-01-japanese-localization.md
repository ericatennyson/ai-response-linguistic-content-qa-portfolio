# Sample 01: Japanese Localization QA

## Source Text

Learn the latest digital transformation strategies for retail companies in this exclusive webinar.

## Literal Translation

この限定ウェビナーで小売企業のための最新のデジタルトランスフォーメーション戦略を学びましょう。

## Improved Japanese

小売業のDX戦略を事例で学ぶ、実務者向けオンラインセミナー

## Issue

The literal translation is understandable, but it sounds generic and slightly unnatural as Japanese B2B marketing copy. 「限定ウェビナー」 and 「デジタルトランスフォーメーション戦略」 are direct and broad, making the value proposition less clear.

## Rationale

The improved version is more concise and better suited for a Japanese B2B audience. It clarifies the target audience by using 「実務者向け」 and makes the value more concrete with 「事例で学ぶ」. This improves both readability and user relevance.

## QA Criteria

- Naturalness: Improved
- Localization quality: Improved
- Audience fit: Improved
- B2B tone fit: Improved
- Content usefulness: Stronger after revision
