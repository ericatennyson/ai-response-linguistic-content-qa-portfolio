# Sample 02: Japanese Business Email Tone QA

## Source Text

Hi, I wanted to follow up and see if you had time to review the proposal.

## Literal Translation

こんにちは、提案を確認する時間があったかフォローアップしたいと思いました。

## Improved Japanese

お世話になっております。  
先日お送りしたご提案資料について、ご確認状況をお伺いできますでしょうか。

## Issue

The literal translation is grammatically understandable, but it does not sound natural in Japanese business communication. 「フォローアップしたいと思いました」 is too direct and translated.

## Rationale

The improved version uses a natural Japanese business email tone. It is polite, concise, and suitable for client communication. It avoids unnecessary directness while clearly asking for the review status.

## QA Criteria

- Naturalness: Improved
- Tone fit: Improved
- Business appropriateness: Improved
- Clarity: Strong
- Linguistic quality: Stronger after revision
