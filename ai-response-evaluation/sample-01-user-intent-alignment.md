# Sample 01: User Intent Alignment

## Prompt

I need a short message to politely decline a freelance project because the rate is too low.

## Response A

You should negotiate aggressively and tell them your rate is non-negotiable. You should also explain your full professional background so they understand your value.

## Response B

Thank you for considering me for this project. After reviewing the scope and rate, I don’t think it is the right fit for me at this time. I appreciate the opportunity and wish you the best with the project.

## Evaluation

**Preferred Response:** Response B

## Rationale

Response B directly addresses the user’s request for a short and polite decline message. It is concise, professional, and ready to use.

Response A shifts the task from declining to negotiating. It also suggests adding unnecessary background information, which does not match the user’s request for a short message.

## Quality Criteria

- User intent alignment: Strong in Response B
- Helpfulness: Strong in Response B
- Conciseness: Strong in Response B
- Tone fit: Strong in Response B
- Task drift: Present in Response A
