# Content QA Rubric

I evaluate content quality based on the following criteria:

## 1. Clarity

Is the message easy to understand?

## 2. Tone

Does the tone match the audience, channel, and purpose?

## 3. Usefulness

Does the content provide clear value to the user or reader?

## 4. Audience Fit

Is the content relevant to the intended audience?

## 5. Message Consistency

Does the landing page or follow-up content match the original promise?

## 6. CTA Quality

Is the call to action clear, specific, and appropriate?

## 7. Claim Quality

Does the content avoid exaggerated, misleading, or unsupported claims?

## 8. Conversion Intent

Does the content support a clear next step without confusing or misleading the user?
