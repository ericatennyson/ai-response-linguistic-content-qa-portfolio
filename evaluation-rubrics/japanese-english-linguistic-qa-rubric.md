# Japanese-English Linguistic QA Rubric

I evaluate Japanese-English linguistic QA based on the following criteria:

## 1. Naturalness

Does the Japanese or English sound natural to native or professional users?

## 2. Accuracy

Does the text preserve the original meaning without adding unsupported information?

## 3. Tone Fit

Does the tone match the product, audience, channel, and context?

## 4. Localization Quality

Does the localized text avoid awkward, overly literal, or culturally mismatched expressions?

## 5. Business Appropriateness

For professional communication, does the text sound polite, clear, and suitable for business use?

## 6. Format Fit

Is the copy appropriate for the format, such as UI, email, landing page, CTA, ad, or business content?

## 7. Bilingual Content Clarity

Does the final text communicate the intended message clearly across Japanese and English contexts?
